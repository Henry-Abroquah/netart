Digital Visions: Exploring the Intersection of Technology and Art

Exhibition curated by Henry Abroquah

In this digital space, I aim to intertwine five captivating topics that delve into technology, society, and art, creating a narrative that explores their interconnectedness.

These works share a temporal and thematic resonance, as they were created during a period marked by rapid technological advancements and societal shifts. They are concerned with exploring the blurred boundaries between reality and artificial constructs, employing innovative aesthetic strategies to challenge perceptions and provoke contemplation. While created by diverse artists from various geographic locations, these works converge to offer a nuanced reflection of our modern world, inviting viewers to engage with the complexities of our digital age.
